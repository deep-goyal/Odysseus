Mobile Development

The journey has been tough. Isn't it! Take some time to reflect on how much you have grown since you were first introduced to Odysseus.

Picture this: smartphones buzzing in everyone's pockets, apps taking center stage, and endless possibilities right at your fingertips. Mobile development is all about crafting the apps that power our everyday lives, whether it's for iOS or Android. From the sleek user interfaces to the nifty functionalities, it's your chance to create something awesome and reach millions of users worldwide. So, grab your coding toolkit and get ready to unleash your creativity in the ever-evolving realm of mobile development. Let's make some magic happen!

iOS App Development

Hey, ever dreamt of building your own app for those shiny iPhones and iPads? Well, welcome to the world of iOS app development! It's all about creating sleek and intuitive apps that make people's lives easier and more fun. With the power of Swift programming language and the robust iOS SDK, you can bring your wildest app ideas to life. From designing captivating interfaces to adding cool features and unleashing your app on the App Store, iOS app development is your ticket to join the thriving community of Apple enthusiasts. So, grab your MacBook, fire up Xcode, and let's embark on this exciting journey of turning your app dreams into reality. Get ready to be the mastermind behind the next big thing in the App Store!

Odysseus firmly believes that the best way to learn app development is to make an app and asking the whys, hows, whats simultaneously. Stanford's CS193p↗ aligns with this philosophy perfectly.

Apple has their own beautiful tutorial↗ to teach beginners the fundamentals of UIKit, SwiftUI and Swift. It is about 15 hours long. Odysseus recommends coupling this doc with the Swift Docs↗ to get a comprehensive understanding of mobile development on iOS.

Android Development

Android development! It's all about crafting apps that run smoothly on a wide range of smartphones and tablets. With the powerful Java or Kotlin programming languages and the versatile Android Studio, you'll have the tools to build apps that captivate users and solve their everyday needs. From designing engaging user interfaces to implementing cool functionalities, Android development lets you unleash your creativity and make a mark in the vast Android ecosystem. So, grab your favorite Android device, fire up Android Studio, and let's embark on this exciting journey of bringing your app ideas to life. Get ready to make a splash in the Google Play Store and create apps that users will love!

Odysseus recommends the Busy Coder's Guide to Android Development↗. This book is clean, comprehensive, and provides several screenshots and code snippets to enhance your understanding of android development.

Project- Mobile App

Odysseus recommends using this GitHub repo↗ or ChatGPT↗ to get an iOS/Android project under your belt. It does not necessarily have to be complicated. Your aim in this project should be getting familiar with the MVVM (Model View ViewModel) architecture and SwiftUI or Kotlin, if you choose to learn Android.
